LoRaWAN®

Welcome to The Things Fundamentals on LoRaWAN!

This series of chapters was created to prepare you for The Things Fundamentals certification. You’ll learn all about LoRa® and LoRaWAN and suitable use cases, and afterwards you’ll be ready to start developing your own LoRaWAN applications.

Info:
The LoRaWAN Fundamentals course can also be followed on Udemy. Sign up for the online course today!
